The CMA OpenLab is a collaborative initiative focused on creative experimentation and open research. We are an inclusive, low-friction creative environment for CMA students and faculty to learn, create and explore. Think of it as a classroom, makerspace, art studio, tinkering workshop, and social hub all under one roof open 24/7. To get started read our code of conduct We maintain a Github Repository with useful code schematics etc. Documentation of the lab and its resources can be found on our Wiki.
